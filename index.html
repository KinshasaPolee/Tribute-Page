Sugar Factory

A family business that started as a small confectionery store.
The Sugar Factory's Timeline:
  • Start - The family business started at the dinner table while discussing candy. They realized candy was the one thing people everyone loves.
  • 2009 - Small confectionery store at the Mirage Hotel in Las Vegas
  • Growth - The brand grew adding a second location that included a cafe. Then they expanded opening a third location and so forth. Presently there are more than 24 locations across the globe.
  • Locations: Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Bahamas, Texas, New York, New Jersey, Mississippi, Minnesota, Las Vegas, Illinois, Georgia, Delaware, Connecticut, Arizona
